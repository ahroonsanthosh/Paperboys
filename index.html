The Front Page

Brunch with ink under its nails.

Tucked on Tobin Street in the heart of Cork City, Paperboys is a brunch room built like an old print shop — cream paper stock, bold blue headlines and a disco ball spinning over the lot.

We keep it classic: eggs the way they should be, coffee pulled properly, and a room that hums from open to close. No fuss, no airs — just the good stuff, served daily.
