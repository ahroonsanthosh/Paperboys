Who we are

A neighbourhood brunch room with a bit of mischief.

Tucked onto Tobin Street, Paperboys is small, warm and unmistakably Cork. We keep the menu tight and the coffee serious — the kind of room you slip into on a slow morning and lose an hour in.

No fuss, no pretence. Just good plates, good people, and our two paperboys keeping an eye on the place. And when the evening calls — we throw a decent party too.
