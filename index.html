Who we are

A neighbourhood brunch room with a bit of mischief.

Small room. Great plates. Serious coffee. Paperboys is tucked onto Tobin Street — the kind of place you plan to stay an hour and end up in all morning.

No fuss. No pretence. Just good food, good people, and our two paperboys keeping watch. When the evening calls — we know how to make it worth your while.
